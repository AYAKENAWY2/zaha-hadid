Crashing news about our blogger “Zaha Hadid” Her words—especially the famous quote, “There are 360 degrees. Why stick to one?”— have stuck with architects in China and designers in Germany and India. Hadid led over sixty major projects during her career. She used cultural context to enrich her work.
