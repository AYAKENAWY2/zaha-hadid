This is Zaha Hadid

The Well-known Iraqi architect. This is my website Portfolio. “When Architecture Met Fashion in the Hands of Zaha Hadid.”She began her own practice in London in 1980 and won the prestigious competition for the Hong Kong Peak Club, a leisure and recreational center in 1983. Painting and drawing, especially in her early period, are important techniques of investigation for her design work. Ever since her 1983 retrospective exhibition at the AA in London, her architecture has been shown in exhibitions worldwide and many of her works are held in important museum collections!
